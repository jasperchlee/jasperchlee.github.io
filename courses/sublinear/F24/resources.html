Mental Health

Being a student can be stressful. If you feel you are under too much pressure or there are psychological issues that are keeping you from performing well at UCD, we encourage you to contact UCD's Student Health and Counseling Services. They provide confidential counselling.

Coping with Unforeseen or Difficult Circumstances

If there are events that are upsetting to you, whether political, family-related, weather-related, etc., that affect your ability to do well in class, we are happy to take them into account with respect to our late policy. Please feel free to talk to the instructor about this. Additionally, the Office of Student Support and Judicial Affairs can be a helpful resource for discussing current concerns and academic and personal plans.
